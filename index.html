Transportation App

Project for a non-profit organization. The organization uses volunteer drivers to provide rides for seniors to places like the grocery store and doctor's office. The app allows the staff to coordinate these rides by matching the information in the request with drivers who have expressed a preference in terms of time and distance. The app is on its way to being using in production.
